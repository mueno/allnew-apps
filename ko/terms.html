3. App Description and Compatibility

The App captures weight scale displays using your camera, reads the numbers using AI-OCR technology, and records them to Apple HealthKit.

  • Supported Scales: Digital displays (7-segment LED, LCD). Analog scales are not supported.
  • Weight Range: 30.0kg–200.0kg (66–440 lbs)
  • Supported Devices: iPhone with iOS 17.0 or later

7. OCR Accuracy

  • No Guarantee: OCR accuracy is not 100%.
  • High-Confidence Auto-Save: If the App determines an OCR result is high confidence, it will be automatically saved to Apple HealthKit without user confirmation.
  • Low-Confidence Confirmation: If the App determines an OCR result is low confidence, it will display the detected value and will record it to HealthKit only if you explicitly confirm that the value is correct. If you do not confirm, the value will not be saved and you will be prompted to retake and retry recognition.
  • How to Correct Entries: Weight entries saved to HealthKit cannot be edited within the App (deletion only). To correct a value, delete the incorrect entry in the iOS Health app and then re-scan and save again using the App.
