Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Este documento está en inglés. La versión traducida al español estará disponible próximamente.
Important: This app does not send or store your weight data or captured images on our external servers. Weight data is stored on your device and in Apple HealthKit. Captured images are discarded immediately after OCR processing. Health data storage/sync behavior (including iCloud) and Apple-side processing depend on your device settings and Apple's system behavior.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera Images: Discarded immediately after OCR processing. Not transmitted to servers operated by us.
  • Health Data (HealthKit): Weight data read/write. Stored on your device and in Apple HealthKit. How Health data may be stored or synced (including via iCloud) depends on your device settings and Apple's system behavior.
  • Device Info: iPhone model identification (for weight subtraction feature). Processed on your device; not transmitted to servers operated by us.
  • Local Storage: Free usage count, purchase status, settings. Not transmitted to servers operated by us.
  • What We Don't Collect: We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics services, or user registration in this App.

2. How We Use Information

Weight recording, health management support, displaying weight trends, wellness messages, and iPhone weight subtraction. No other purposes.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not provide, sell, or share image data or HealthKit data with third parties.

4. Apple HealthKit

  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising, marketing, or data mining
  • HealthKit data is not sold to data brokers
  • We do not transmit or store HealthKit data on our own external servers. How Health data may be stored or synced (including via iCloud) depends on your device settings and Apple's system behavior
  • HealthKit access can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings

5. Apple Intelligence (On-Device AI)

The App may use Apple Intelligence for wellness messages. Processing typically occurs on-device; however, depending on Apple's system behavior, some requests may be processed via Private Cloud Compute. We do not transmit or store your data on our own external servers for this processing.

6. Security

We employ data minimization, on-device processing where possible, Keychain protection, and HealthKit encryption. When Apple Intelligence is used, some requests may be processed via Private Cloud Compute depending on Apple's system behavior.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not collect personal information through the App's functionality.

8. Your Rights

  • All Users: Manage/delete HealthKit data via Settings > Health. When uninstalling, iOS asks whether to delete health data.
  • EU/EEA (GDPR): Right to lodge complaints, withdraw consent.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know, delete, opt-out. We don't sell personal information.
  • China (PIPL): Right to know, access, correct, delete. We do not transfer your data to our external servers. However, depending on your Apple/iCloud settings and Apple services (e.g., iCloud sync, Private Cloud Compute), data may be processed or stored outside your country/region.
  • South Korea (PIPA): Right to request processing suspension.
  • Brazil (LGPD): Right to data portability.

9. When You Contact Us

If you contact us, we may receive your email address and the contents of your message to respond to your inquiry. We use this information solely for customer support and do not share it with third parties except as required by law. We retain it only for a reasonable period necessary to handle your request.
