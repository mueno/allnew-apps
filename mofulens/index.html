Privacy Policy
How we handle your data
Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Important: This app does not send or store your video or image data on our external servers. All data is processed on your device.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera Feed: Used only for real-time ARKit body tracking. Video is not recorded or transmitted; each frame is processed on-device and discarded.
  • Photo Library: Accessed only when saving snapshots. Saved to your device's Camera Roll.
  • Local Storage: Free usage count, purchase status, settings. Not transmitted to servers operated by us.
  • What We Don't Collect: We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics services, or user registration. No facial recognition data or biometric information is collected or stored.

2. How We Use Information

Real-time AR costume display and snapshot saving. No other purposes.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not provide, sell, or share camera footage, snapshots, or any other data with third parties.

4. Face & Biometric Information

  • This app uses ARKit Body Tracking (body detection) but does not use facial recognition (Face ID, TrueDepth, etc.)
  • No biometric authentication data is collected, stored, or transmitted
  • Costumes are designed to completely cover the entire face, so snapshots never expose facial features

5. Security

We employ data minimization, on-device real-time processing, and Keychain protection for sensitive information.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not collect personal information through the App's functionality.

7. Your Rights

  • All Users: Deleting the app removes all on-device settings. Snapshots saved to Camera Roll can be managed through the Photos app.
  • EU/EEA (GDPR): Right to lodge complaints, withdraw consent.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know, delete, opt-out. We don't sell personal information.
  • China (PIPL): Right to know, access, correct, delete. We do not transfer your data to servers operated by us outside your country.
  • South Korea (PIPA): Right to request processing suspension.
  • Brazil (LGPD): Right to data portability.

8. When You Contact Us

If you contact us, we may receive your email address and the contents of your message to respond to your inquiry. We use this information solely for customer support and do not share it with third parties except as required by law.

9. Governing Law

This policy is governed by Japanese law. Regional laws (GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, PIPL, LGPD, PIPA) apply to respective residents.

10. Translations
