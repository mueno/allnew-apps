Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Important: This app does not send or store your weight data on external servers. All data is stored only on your device and in Apple HealthKit.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera Images: Discarded immediately after OCR processing. Never sent externally.
  • Health Data (HealthKit): Weight data read/write. Stored on device only.
  • Device Info: iPhone model identification (for weight subtraction feature). On-device only.
  • Local Storage: Free usage count, purchase status, settings. Never sent externally.
  • What We Don't Collect: No advertising SDKs, no analytics, no user registration.

2. How We Use Information

Weight recording, health management support, displaying weight trends, wellness messages, and iPhone weight subtraction. No other purposes.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not provide, sell, or share image data or HealthKit data with third parties.

4. Apple HealthKit

  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising, marketing, or data mining
  • HealthKit data is not sold to data brokers
  • HealthKit data is not stored on external servers including iCloud
  • HealthKit access can be revoked anytime in iOS Settings

5. Apple Intelligence (On-Device AI)

The App may use Apple Intelligence to generate wellness messages. All processing occurs on-device with no external transmission.

6. Security

We employ data minimization, on-device processing, Keychain protection, and HealthKit encryption.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13. We do not collect personal information from anyone.

8. Your Rights

  • All Users: Manage/delete HealthKit data via Settings > Health. When uninstalling, iOS asks whether to delete health data.
  • EU/EEA (GDPR): Right to lodge complaints, withdraw consent.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know, delete, opt-out. We don't sell personal information.
  • China (PIPL): Right to know, access, correct, delete. No cross-border transfer.
  • South Korea (PIPA): Right to request processing suspension.
  • Brazil (LGPD): Right to data portability.
