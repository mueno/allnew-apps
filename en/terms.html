These Terms of Service ("Terms") govern your use of the iOS application "WeightSnap" ("App") provided by AllNew LLC ("Company", "we", "us"). By using the App, you agree to these Terms.

1. About the App

  1. The App captures a photo of your scale display, uses OCR to estimate a weight value, and—based on your permissions and settings—writes weight data to the Apple Health app (via HealthKit).
  2. If you optionally grant read access, the App may also read your weight data from the Apple Health app to show changes since your previous measurement and to display trends (e.g., charts/graphs).
  3. The App is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Supported Scales: Digital displays (7-segment LED, LCD). Analog scales are not supported.
Weight Range: 30.0kg–200.0kg (66–440 lbs)
Supported Devices: iPhone with iOS 17.0 or later

2. Recognition Confidence and Saving

  1. The App determines a confidence level for the recognition result:
    • Default behavior: the App shows the detected value and writes to Apple Health only after you approve by tapping "Record". If you do not approve, nothing is saved and you can retry.
    • With Auto-Save enabled: If you enable automatic recording in settings, the App may write high-confidence results to Apple Health without additional confirmation.
  2. We do not guarantee perfect accuracy.

3. Correcting Entries in Apple Health

Entries in Apple Health may not be editable and may only be deletable depending on Apple's system behavior. To correct a value, delete it in Apple Health and re-record in the App.

4. Data Transmission & Storage (Key Point)

  1. We do not transmit or store your weight data, Apple Health read data, captured images, or motion data on servers operated by us.
  2. If read access is granted, the App uses the read data only for on-device display and analysis (e.g., deltas and charts).
  3. Captured images are used temporarily for OCR and are not transmitted or stored on servers operated by us.
  4. We do not use any analytics SDK in the App.
